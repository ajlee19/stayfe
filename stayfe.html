Everyday there are all kinds of crimes happening around us. Our mission is to provide the most comprehensive real time crime data so that our users can easily visualize the risk of any location.

Overview

We collected data from local news media and compared them with official police reports. Crime data published by police departments may take weeks to be updated and often cover only specific locations. By adding crime data crawled from credible news articles and processing them with various machine learning techniques, we provide the most comprehensive and up-to-date content to our users. Red dots are collected from crawling news media and the yellow dots are from the police data base.
